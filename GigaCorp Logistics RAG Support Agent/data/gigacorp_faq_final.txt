GigaCorp Customer Support Knowledge Base
=========================================

SECTION 1: SHIPPING POLICY
1.1 GigaCorp ships to over 40 countries worldwide, including the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, Germany, Australia, and India.
1.2 Standard shipping within the continental United States takes 5-7 business days and costs $4.99, or is free on orders over $50.
1.3 Express shipping within the United States takes 1-2 business days and costs $14.99.
1.4 International shipping to India, Australia, and the UK takes 10-14 business days and costs $19.99 per order.
1.5 International express shipping is available to select countries (UK, Canada, Germany) for $39.99 and takes 3-5 business days.
1.6 Orders are processed within 24 hours on business days. Orders placed after 3 PM EST are processed the next business day.
1.7 GigaCorp does not currently ship to P.O. boxes for orders containing electronics.
1.8 Customers can track their shipment using the tracking link emailed at the time of dispatch.
1.9 GigaCorp does not ship to APO/FPO military addresses at this time, but this is under review for a future rollout.
1.10 Orders over 20 lbs (9 kg) combined weight are automatically routed to freight shipping, which adds 2-4 extra business days.
1.11 Same-day delivery is available in Austin, Dallas, and Houston for orders placed before 11 AM local time, for a flat fee of $9.99.
1.12 During the November-December holiday season, the last guaranteed delivery date for standard shipping is December 18, and for express shipping is December 22.
1.13 Signature-on-delivery is automatically required for orders over $300 and cannot be waived.
1.14 Shipping addresses can be changed only if the order has not yet entered the "Processing" status; once processed, the address is locked.
1.15 GigaCorp partners with FedEx, UPS, and USPS for domestic shipping, and DHL for international shipping.
1.16 Split shipments (when items ship separately due to warehouse location) do not incur additional shipping charges.

SECTION 2: RETURN AND REFUND PROCESS
2.1 GigaCorp accepts returns within 30 days of the delivery date for a full refund.
2.2 Items must be unused, in original packaging, with all tags attached to qualify for a return.
2.3 To initiate a return, customers must log into their GigaCorp account, go to "Order History," and select "Start a Return."
2.4 Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after GigaCorp receives the returned item.
2.5 Refunds are issued to the original payment method. GigaCorp does not offer cash refunds.
2.6 Return shipping is free for defective or incorrect items. For all other returns, a $5.99 return shipping fee is deducted from the refund.
2.7 Final sale items (marked "Clearance" or "Final Sale") are not eligible for return or exchange.
2.8 Exchanges are processed as a return plus a new order, and are subject to standard shipping times.
2.9 Items purchased using store credit or a gift card are refunded back to store credit, not the original payment method.
2.10 A restocking fee of 10% applies to opened large appliances (refrigerators, washing machines, and similar items).
2.11 Return labels are valid for 14 days from the date they are generated; expired labels must be reissued through support.
2.12 International returns are the customer's responsibility to ship back and are not covered by GigaCorp's free return shipping policy.
2.13 Items marked as "Final Sale" can still be returned if they arrive defective or damaged in transit.
2.14 GigaCorp does not accept returns of opened personal care items (headphones with worn ear tips, razors, etc.) for hygiene reasons.
2.15 A return can be converted into store credit for an extra 10% bonus value instead of a refund to the original payment method.

SECTION 3: BUSINESS HOURS AND CONTACT
3.1 GigaCorp's customer support team is available Monday through Friday, 8 AM to 8 PM EST, and Saturday, 10 AM to 4 PM EST.
3.2 GigaCorp customer support is closed on Sundays and major US public holidays.
3.3 Customers can reach support via live chat on the website, email at support@gigacorp-example.com, or phone at 1-800-555-0199.
3.4 Average live chat response time is under 2 minutes during business hours.
3.5 Average email response time is within 24 hours on business days.
3.6 GigaCorp's corporate headquarters is located in Austin, Texas, and warehouses are located in Ohio, Nevada, and New Jersey.
3.7 GigaCorp also offers support via X (formerly Twitter) at @GigaCorpHelp and via WhatsApp Business for Premium tier members only.
3.8 Phone wait times during peak hours (12 PM-2 PM EST) can be up to 15 minutes; callback requests are available to avoid waiting on hold.
3.9 Support is offered in English, Spanish, and French; other languages are supported via a translation tool with a brief delay.
3.10 The support team does not process order changes or cancellations over social media for security reasons - these must go through chat, email, or phone.

SECTION 4: SERVICE TIERS AND MEMBERSHIP
4.1 GigaCorp offers three service tiers: Basic, Plus, and Premium.
4.2 The Basic tier is free and includes standard shipping rates and standard 5-7 business day customer support response times.
4.3 The Plus tier costs $9.99/month and includes free standard shipping on all orders, priority email support, and a 45-day return window instead of 30 days.
4.4 The Premium tier costs $19.99/month and includes free express shipping on all domestic orders, 24/7 live chat support, a 60-day return window, and early access to sales.
4.5 Premium members receive a dedicated account manager for orders over $500.
4.6 Members can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel their tier at any time from Account Settings; downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
4.7 Annual billing for Plus and Premium tiers gives customers a 15% discount compared to monthly billing.
4.8 A free 30-day trial of Premium is available to new accounts and automatically converts to Basic if not upgraded before the trial ends.
4.9 Family Plans allow up to 5 linked accounts to share one Premium membership for $29.99/month total.
4.10 Students with a valid .edu email address receive 50% off Plus or Premium tier pricing.
4.11 Membership tiers do not affect product prices, only shipping speed, support access, and return windows.

SECTION 5: PRODUCT WARRANTY
5.1 All electronics sold by GigaCorp come with a standard 1-year manufacturer warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship.
5.2 Premium tier members receive an extended 2-year warranty at no additional cost on eligible electronics.
5.3 Warranty claims can be filed through the "Warranty Support" tab in the customer's account dashboard.
5.4 Physical damage, water damage, and unauthorized repairs are not covered under the standard or extended warranty.
5.5 GigaCorp Protect, an optional add-on purchased at checkout for 8% of item price, covers accidental damage for 2 years including drops and spills.
5.6 Furniture and home goods carry a 5-year structural warranty covering frame and joint defects, but not fabric wear or staining.
5.7 Warranty replacement items ship free regardless of membership tier and typically arrive within 5-7 business days of claim approval.
5.8 A proof of purchase (order number or receipt) is required for all warranty claims; GigaCorp cannot process claims without one.
5.9 Refurbished items sold in the GigaCorp Outlet carry a reduced 90-day warranty instead of the standard 1-year term.

SECTION 6: PAYMENT AND BILLING
6.1 GigaCorp accepts Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, and Apple Pay.
6.2 GigaCorp does not store full credit card numbers; payment processing is handled through a PCI-compliant third-party processor.
6.3 Sales tax is calculated based on the shipping destination and applied at checkout.
6.4 Promo codes can only be applied at checkout and cannot be added retroactively to a completed order.
6.5 GigaCorp also accepts Google Pay and, for orders under $1,000, Affirm and Klarna installment financing.
6.6 Gift cards can be combined with one other payment method but cannot be used to purchase additional gift cards.
6.7 Failed payments due to insufficient funds place the order on a 24-hour hold before automatically canceling if not resolved.
6.8 Currency is charged in USD regardless of shipping destination; international customers may see foreign transaction fees from their bank.
6.9 Corporate/business accounts can request net-30 invoicing after a credit check, available only for orders over $2,000.
6.10 Price adjustments for items that drop in price within 7 days of purchase can be requested once per order via support.

SECTION 7: ORDER QUANTITIES AND BULK PURCHASES
7.1 Standard consumer accounts are limited to a maximum of 10 units of the same SKU per order to prevent reseller abuse.
7.2 Orders containing more than 10 units of one item are automatically flagged and may be canceled with a refund unless placed through a Business Account.
7.3 During major sales events (Black Friday, Cyber Monday), the per-SKU limit is temporarily reduced to 3 units to ensure fair access.
7.4 Bulk orders of 50+ units of a single item qualify for tiered volume discounts: 5% off at 50 units, 10% off at 150 units, and 15% off at 300+ units.
7.5 Bulk and wholesale orders must be requested through the Business Accounts team at business@gigacorp-example.com rather than the standard checkout.
7.6 There is no minimum order quantity for standard purchases; single-unit orders are always accepted.
7.7 Maximum order value for a single transaction on a standard account is $5,000; larger purchases must be split into multiple orders or use invoicing.
7.8 Quantity limits reset immediately after an order is placed and are tracked per account, not per household or address.

SECTION 8: ORDER MODIFICATIONS AND CANCELLATIONS
8.1 Orders can be canceled free of charge within 60 minutes of being placed, as long as they have not entered "Processing" status.
8.2 Once an order reaches "Processing" status, it cannot be canceled or modified; the customer must wait for delivery and initiate a return instead.
8.3 Quantity changes, size/color swaps, and address updates can be requested within the same 60-minute cancellation window.
8.4 Orders paid for with financing (Affirm/Klarna) can only be canceled by contacting support directly, not through self-service tools.
8.5 If an order is accidentally duplicated, GigaCorp automatically flags and refunds the duplicate within 24 hours without requiring a support ticket.
8.6 Gift orders can be canceled by either the purchaser or the recipient if the recipient was given order access via a shared gift link.

SECTION 9: INTERNATIONAL ORDERS, CUSTOMS & DUTIES
9.1 International customers are responsible for any customs duties, import taxes, or VAT assessed by their local government upon arrival.
9.2 GigaCorp does not pre-pay customs duties; these are collected by the shipping carrier at the time of delivery.
9.3 Estimated customs fees are shown at checkout for reference but are not guaranteed to match the final amount charged by customs authorities.
9.4 Orders refused at customs due to unpaid duties are returned to GigaCorp and refunded minus the original outbound shipping cost.
9.5 Certain electronics with lithium batteries cannot be shipped to some countries due to international air freight restrictions; affected items are marked at checkout.
9.6 International order tracking may show limited detail once the package leaves the origin country, depending on the destination carrier.

SECTION 10: GIFT CARDS AND STORE CREDIT
10.1 GigaCorp gift cards are available in denominations of $10, $25, $50, $100, and $250, and never expire.
10.2 Digital gift cards are delivered by email within 15 minutes of purchase; physical gift cards ship within standard shipping timeframes.
10.3 Gift card balances can be checked at any time under Account Settings > Gift Cards, no login required if the code is known.
10.4 Store credit issued from a return does not expire and can be used toward any future purchase, including shipping fees.
10.5 Lost or stolen physical gift cards can be deactivated and reissued only if the original purchase receipt is provided.
10.6 Gift cards cannot be redeemed for cash except where required by state law.

SECTION 11: LOYALTY REWARDS PROGRAM
11.1 The GigaCorp Rewards program is free to join and earns 1 point per $1 spent, with 100 points redeemable for a $5 reward.
11.2 Premium tier members earn 2x points on every purchase compared to Basic and Plus members.
11.3 Points expire 18 months after the date they are earned if the account remains inactive during that period.
11.4 Birthday month bonuses give all Rewards members double points on purchases made during their registered birthday month.
11.5 Rewards points cannot be combined with gift card balances in a single redemption, but can be combined with promo codes.
11.6 Referring a friend who makes a first purchase over $25 earns both the referrer and the new customer a $10 reward credit.

SECTION 12: PRODUCT CATEGORIES AND INVENTORY
12.1 GigaCorp's catalog spans six main categories: Electronics, Home & Furniture, Kitchen & Appliances, Outdoor & Garden, Apparel, and Office Supplies.
12.2 Product availability is shown in real time on each listing page; "Low Stock" labels appear when fewer than 10 units remain.
12.3 Out-of-region items (available only in certain countries due to voltage or certification differences) are clearly marked on the product page.
12.4 GigaCorp Outlet features refurbished and open-box items at 20-40% off original price, inspected and certified by GigaCorp's technical team.
12.5 New product arrivals are added to the catalog every Tuesday and Friday.

SECTION 13: CORPORATE AND WHOLESALE ACCOUNTS
13.1 Business Accounts are free to create and unlock bulk pricing, net-30 invoicing eligibility, and a dedicated business support line.
13.2 Business Accounts require a valid business tax ID or reseller certificate to be verified within 3-5 business days of signup.
13.3 Wholesale pricing tiers are only visible after a Business Account has been verified and logged in.
13.4 Business Accounts can generate itemized invoices in PDF format for accounting purposes directly from Order History.
13.5 A minimum annual spend of $10,000 qualifies a Business Account for a dedicated account manager, similar to Premium consumer tier benefits.

SECTION 14: PROMOTIONS, DISCOUNTS & COUPONS
14.1 Only one promo code can be applied per order, except during select "stackable promo" events announced via email.
14.2 Promo codes generally exclude GigaCorp Outlet, gift cards, and warranty add-ons like GigaCorp Protect.
14.3 Newsletter subscribers receive a 10% off code for their first order, valid for 30 days from signup.
14.4 Flash sales are limited to 2 units per customer regardless of the standard 10-unit order quantity limit.
14.5 Expired promo codes cannot be reactivated or honored retroactively, even if the expiration was close to the order date.

SECTION 15: SUBSCRIPTIONS AND AUTO-REORDER
15.1 Eligible consumable products (filters, cleaning supplies, pet food) can be set to Auto-Reorder on a 30, 60, or 90-day cycle.
15.2 Auto-Reorder subscribers save an additional 5% on every recurring shipment compared to one-time purchase price.
15.3 Auto-Reorder shipments can be skipped, rescheduled, or canceled at any time from Account Settings with no penalty.
15.4 Customers are notified by email 3 days before an Auto-Reorder charge is processed, allowing time to make changes.
15.5 If an Auto-Reorder payment fails twice, the subscription is automatically paused until payment details are updated.

SECTION 16: PRICE MATCHING AND ADJUSTMENTS
16.1 GigaCorp will match a lower price found on Amazon.com or Walmart.com for an identical, in-stock item within 7 days of purchase.
16.2 Price match requests must be submitted through support with a link or screenshot of the competitor's listing showing price and availability.
16.3 Price matching does not apply to third-party marketplace sellers, clearance items, or limited-time flash sales.
16.4 Approved price match adjustments are issued as a partial refund to the original payment method within 3-5 business days.

SECTION 17: BACKORDERS AND OUT-OF-STOCK ITEMS
17.1 Out-of-stock items can be pre-ordered as a backorder, charging the payment method only when the item ships.
17.2 Estimated restock dates are shown on the product page and updated weekly based on supplier data.
17.3 Backordered items in a multi-item order ship separately by default so in-stock items are not delayed.
17.4 Customers can cancel a backordered item at any time before it ships with no fee.
17.5 If a backordered item does not restock within 60 days, GigaCorp automatically cancels the backorder and notifies the customer.

SECTION 18: DAMAGED, LOST, OR STOLEN PACKAGES
18.1 Packages that arrive visibly damaged should be reported within 48 hours of delivery with photos for a replacement or refund.
18.2 Packages marked "delivered" by the carrier but not received should be reported within 5 days; GigaCorp will file a carrier trace investigation.
18.3 GigaCorp offers free replacement, not just a refund, for orders confirmed lost or stolen in transit, at customer request.
18.4 Signature-required orders (over $300) that are marked delivered but disputed are investigated directly with the carrier before any resolution is issued.
18.5 Porch piracy (theft after confirmed delivery) is covered once per account per rolling 12-month period as a courtesy replacement.

SECTION 19: ACCOUNT SECURITY AND PRIVACY
19.1 GigaCorp supports two-factor authentication (2FA) via SMS or authenticator app, which is required automatically for Business Accounts.
19.2 Customer payment data is never shared with third-party marketers; GigaCorp's privacy policy is available at gigacorp-example.com/privacy.
19.3 Password reset links expire after 30 minutes and can only be used once.
19.4 Customers can request full deletion of their account and associated data by submitting a request through Account Settings > Privacy, processed within 30 days.
19.5 Suspicious login attempts trigger an automatic email alert and temporary account lock until identity is verified.

SECTION 20: MOBILE APP AND TECHNICAL SUPPORT
20.1 The GigaCorp app is available for iOS 15+ and Android 10+, and supports order tracking, push notifications, and mobile-exclusive flash sales.
20.2 App-exclusive discounts of an additional 5% are occasionally offered and are not available on the desktop website.
20.3 Technical issues with the app or website (payment errors, login failures) can be reported through the in-app "Report a Problem" tool for fastest response.
20.4 The GigaCorp app supports barcode scanning in physical partner retail locations to check online pricing and availability.
20.5 Push notifications for order updates can be disabled at any time from the app's Notification Settings without affecting email updates.

SECTION 21: ACCESSIBILITY
21.1 GigaCorp's website and app are designed to meet WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility standards, including screen reader compatibility.
21.2 Customers with accessibility needs can request an accessible-format invoice or order confirmation (large print or audio-described) through support.
21.3 A dedicated accessibility support line is available at 1-800-555-0175 during standard business hours.

SECTION 22: SUSTAINABILITY AND PACKAGING
22.1 GigaCorp uses 100% recyclable cardboard packaging and has eliminated single-use plastic air pillows as of last year.
22.2 Customers can opt in to "Green Shipping," which consolidates multi-item orders into fewer boxes even if it adds 1-2 extra delivery days.
22.3 Electronics returned for warranty replacement are refurbished or responsibly recycled rather than discarded when possible.
22.4 A tree is planted through GigaCorp's reforestation partner for every order over $75, at no extra cost to the customer.
